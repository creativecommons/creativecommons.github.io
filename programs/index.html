Programs

Creative Commons (CC) regularly participates in the following programs that offer grants or stipends:

Google Season of Docs (GSoD)

Creative Commons is not currently participating in Google Season of Docs.

  • Program website

Google Summer of Code (GSoC)

2022 Organization application in progress.

  • Program website

Outreachy

Creative Commons is participating in the 2021 End-of-year / December Cohort of Outreachy.

Creative Commons is not participating in the May 2022 to August 2022 Outreachy internship round.

  • Program website
