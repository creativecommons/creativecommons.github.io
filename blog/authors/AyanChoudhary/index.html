Ayan Choudhary

Ayan Choudhary is an Electrical Engineering undergraduate student from India and interned Creative Commons during the summer of 2020. He has been involved with coding quite heavily for the past couple of years which is one of his numerous hobbies. Some of the sectors which really fascinate him include network security, blockchain, and data science. Apart from this he loves reading and painting and is quite interested in PC gaming and binge-watching online shows.

He worked on ccsearch as a part of GSoC 2020.

He is @ayan on slack.
